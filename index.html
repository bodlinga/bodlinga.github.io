About

I’m a CFD engineer and developer specializing in rotorcraft and high-performance computing. I’ve spent over a decade building, extending, and applying high-fidelity solvers for the U.S. Department of Defense, NASA, and industry partners—covering everything from rotorcraft wake aerodynamics to store-separation and tiltrotor / ship-deck interactions.

My work bridges numerical methods, GPU performance engineering, and production workflows. I enjoy taking complex, legacy CFD ecosystems and turning them into robust, performance-portable tools—integrating modern C++, Pybind11, and GPU backends while keeping analysts productive and simulations tightly validated against wind-tunnel and flight-test data.
